About Us

Mpondo Busy Corner & Chesanyama is located in Fochville, Gauteng, and is known for its vibrant atmosphere, chesanyama grills, and social energy. On Google Maps, customers have rated it 4.6/5.

Whether you're planning a casual meal, a late-night outing, or a weekend meetup, the venue offers a local township-style experience with takeaway and sit-down options.
